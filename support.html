Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app → Profile tab → Delete account. Your auth row, listings, and outgoing swap requests are deleted immediately. Backups are purged within 30 days.

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. We'll send a reset link that opens the app and lets you set a new password.

How do I report a listing or message?

Tap the listing or swap request, then tap the "..." menu → Report. We aim to act on reports within 24 hours.

How do I block a member?

Tap the member's name on a listing or swap request, then tap "Block". Blocked members will not see your listings or be able to send you swap requests.

How do I cancel a subscription?

iOS subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Apple Account → Subscriptions to cancel. The Profile tab also has a direct link.

Is Keyaway responsible for my swap?

No. Keyaway is a planning tool, not a party to any swap. We do not collect fees, provide insurance, or perform background checks. Please read our Terms of Service for details on liability and member responsibilities.
