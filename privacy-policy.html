Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-05-08 · Last updated: 2026-05-09

This Privacy Policy describes how Keyaway ("Keyaway", "we", "us") handles personal information when you use the Keyaway iOS app and related services. Keyaway is operated by Satish Nagabhyrava as an independent developer.

What we collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, password (stored only as a salted hash by our auth provider).
  • Listing content you create: title, city, country, description, photos, availability dates, and approximate coordinates of homes you list. This is User Generated Content visible to other Keyaway members once you publish a listing.
  • Swap requests: dates and a free-text message you send to a host. Visible to that host and to you.
  • Device location: only when you grant the iOS "When in Use" permission, used solely to plot homes on the map. Keyaway never tracks location in the background.
  • Photos: only when you choose to add photos to a listing.
  • Standard server-side metadata: timestamps and Supabase-side request logs.
  • Records of your acceptance of legal disclaimers: which disclaimer you agreed to (e.g., lease/HOA, short-term-rental laws, insurance, recording devices, vehicles, age 18+ to host, ToS click-wrap), the version of the disclaimer copy, and the timestamp of acceptance. Retained for as long as your account exists for dispute purposes.
  • Account identifier: a stable Supabase user UUID linked to your account. Used internally to route requests and apply row-level security; never shared with other users beyond what's necessary to complete a swap interaction.

What we do not collect

  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or fingerprinting SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA.
  • We do not request your DOB.

Who processes your data

Account, listing, and swap-request data are stored in Supabase, our infrastructure provider. Apple App Store handles purchase data when applicable. Email delivery for password resets goes through Supabase's transactional mail provider.

International data transfers

Keyaway is operated from the United States, and Supabase processes data in U.S.-based infrastructure (AWS US-East). If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, your personal data is transferred to the U.S. for processing. Where the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (DPF) applies, that mechanism governs the transfer; where DPF coverage is unavailable, transfers rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (Module 2 — controller to processor) executed with Supabase.

Schrems II considerations: we restrict access to U.S. infrastructure to those with a legitimate operational need, encrypt data at rest and in transit, and have documented our transfer impact assessment. EEA, UK, and Swiss residents may exercise rights under the GDPR (Articles 15–22) by emailing nagabs66@gmail.com: right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, objection, and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

How long we keep it

For as long as your account exists. When you delete your account from inside the app (Profile → Delete account), we delete your auth row, listings, and outgoing swap requests immediately. Records of your acceptance of legal disclaimers are retained for the duration of your account for dispute purposes and are deleted on account deletion. Backups are purged within 30 days.

Your rights

  • Access & export: email nagabs66@gmail.com to receive a JSON export of your data.
  • Deletion: use Profile → Delete account in the app, or email the address above.
  • Correction: edit your profile and listings inside the app. For anything else, email us.
  • Do Not Sell or Share: Keyaway does not sell or share personal information.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the CPRA:

  • Right to Know — request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of your personal information (subject to limited exceptions, including ongoing dispute records under §15 of our Terms).
  • Right to Correct — request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information — direct us to limit the use and disclosure of any sensitive personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not retaliate against you for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise any CCPA right, email nagabs66@gmail.com. We will verify your identity using the email address associated with your Keyaway account and respond within 45 days. Keyaway does not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined in the CCPA, and we do not use personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Children

Keyaway is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in violation of the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to Keyaway, please contact nagabs66@gmail.com.

Separately, Keyaway is rated 17+ on the App Store, and we require a self-affirmation that you are 17 or older to use the app. Hosting (publishing a listing) requires a separate self-affirmation that you are 18 or older, since most U.S. states require legal adulthood to enter binding home-occupancy arrangements.
